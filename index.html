Artist Matías Delóngaro

Matías was born in Mendoza, Argentina. The 32 years old boy, has began with the production of electronic music and Dj in 2017. He was always characterized by the breadth of genres, and over the years he acquired and created his own sound with influences from Progressive House, Deep House, Organic House and more. Growing little by little and creating his style, always with the characteristic of merging different genres.
